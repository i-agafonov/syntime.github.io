SwissCoin Digital Currency

Why exactly Switzerland?

Switzerland is a country with high potential development of cryptocurrency in the financial market. Already in 2014 in Switzerland Bitcoin equated to foreign currency and is a means carry out commercial transactions. This summer came into force a decision on the establishment of SBEX. In this regard, all banks in Switzerland are now taking deposits in bitcoin, and the owner of cryptocurrency can get the money in Swiss francs at local ATMs. Switzerland is becoming one of the most favorable countries for Bitcoin. Because of the increased interest in Bitcoin in the financial market of Switzerland, cryptocurrency is beginning to become increasingly popular and is taking legitimacy.

The Swiss Confederation is a country with the ancient traditions of the highest level in the field of financial and banking services. The largest and most ancient financial conglomerates and banks in Europe, such as, UBS, Schweizerische Nationalbank, Credit Suisse, Raiffeisen are here. These banks have proven their reliability and stability more than once, so the richest people of the all world have accounts in Swiss banks. The banking system is the developed, accurate and well coordinated "financial mechanism." Occurring every second transaction with the customer's bank account, a transfer of money from one account to another, the exchange of one currency for another, the discovery of new deposits and accounts, payment transactions with credit and other... All this is very laborious, spends a lot of time and energy. Currency SwissCoin created to simplify bank operations and working with clients. The single currency for the entire network of the Swiss banks will let quickly execute transactions and transfers. Bank operations with customer deposits will considerably simplified. Also throughout the network of payment terminals in Switzerland, and in the terminals Bitaccess will be the payment currency SwissCoin.
